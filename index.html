    Q: What frequencies do your towers emit?
    A: 5Ghz


    Q: What kind of router do I need?
    A: We recommend a dual band router that is capable of pulling the speeds that you sign up for. It is also suggested to ensure the router has ample coverage for the square footage of your home


    Q: How big is the dish?
    A: About 12" in diameter


    Q: How does weather affect these services?
    A: Normal weather patterns typically do not affect our services. However, weather conditions such as; heavy rains, snow, or ice can potentially affect the services as they may prevent the radio waves from being transmitted or received
